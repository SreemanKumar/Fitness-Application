Our Equipments

Smith Machine

A guided barbell system ideal for safe squats, bench presses, and shoulder presses, providing stability and support for heavy lifting workouts.

Power Rack

A versatile setup allowing free-weight exercises like squats, deadlifts, and bench presses, equipped with adjustable safety bars for secure and solo lifting.

Leg Press Machine

An advanced lower body workout tool targeting quads, hamstrings, and glutes, offering controlled resistance and customizable weight options for strength building.

Battle Ropes

Dynamic fitness equipment for high-intensity interval training, improving endurance, core stability, and grip strength through rhythmic, full-body movements.

Assault Bike (Air Bike)

Combines cardio and strength training with air resistance increasing effort proportionally, making it perfect for intense, calorie-burning workouts.

Functional Trainer

A cable-based machine offering full-body, multi-directional resistance exercises, enhancing functional strength, core stability, and movement versatility.
